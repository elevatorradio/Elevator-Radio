ELEVATOR RADIO

Elevator Radio is a radio network run by students from Design Academy Eindhoven. Established as an umbrella network to bring together existing and new radio initiatives, Elevator Radio acts as an experimental platform for voices from the student community. A platform for students to collaborate and play with ideas in the medium of live broadcasting and audio.

Elevator radio is supported by Design Academy Eindhoven.



20th May Broadcast Schedule



16:00-17:00 INTRO HOUR w/ Ingrid, Nick and Louisa

During Intro Hour we will be giving a special introduction to Elevator Radio - how we came about, our visual identity and plans for the future. We will squeeze in some elevator ups and downs, unusual weather forecasts, and there will also be an opportunity for you to join us live on air to share any radio show ideas you may have.

17:00-18:00 AROUND DAE w/ Jess

During this second hour we will take you on a journey around the world of DAE! From the food at the ZBar to our everyday language that makes us unique. You are also invited to join us on air again to share any radio show ideas you have. Featuring special guests: Mika from the ZBar and Matilde from the Critical Inquiry Lab.

18:00-19:00 DIALOGUE HOUR w/ Jasper and Val

Dialogue Hour will bring you a fine selection of small talk. From, what’s occurring on Student Affairs, to experimental tracks from the DAE community, to Discomfort Dialogues.We will also talk about plans for the Roaming Radio, a portable nomad trolley that will be bringing Elevator Radio to every remote corner of DAE!

19:00-20:00 FLAM-SCHLANGE w/ Felix, Nick and Louisa

Maybe you've already heard of the Sunday Breakfast Club w/Polarwolf or the ididididid DJs. Well, Louisa, Nick and Felix have now joined forces to form the DJ trio ‘Flam-Schlange’!
