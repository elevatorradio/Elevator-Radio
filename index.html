ABOUT

Elevator Radio is a radio network run by students from Design Academy Eindhoven. Established as an umbrella network to bring together existing and new radio initiatives, Elevator Radio acts as an experimental platform for voices from the student community. A platform for students to collaborate and play with ideas in the medium of live broadcasting and audio.

Elevator Radio is supported by Design Academy Eindhoven.
